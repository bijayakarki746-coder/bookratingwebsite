ABOUT THIS LOG

Who I am & why I read

👤

A reader, mostly

I read widely and slowly. Fiction, mostly — literary, speculative, historical — but also essays, memoirs, and the occasional philosophy text I never quite finish. Reading is the most efficient way to spend time inside another person's mind.

📖

Why this log exists

Books disappear from memory faster than we admit. This log holds onto them — the quotes that winded me, the arguments I'm still turning over. If a book is worth reading it is worth recording.

"A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies. The man who never reads lives only one." — George R.R. Martin
